Red Flags I Catch

Real issues found in recent engagements

‚ö†

Claim Mismatch

"Proprietary AI" that's actually a thin wrapper around OpenAI APIs. "Real-time processing" that's batch jobs with 15-minute delays. Pitch deck vs. production reality gaps.

‚ö†

Security Debt

Hardcoded credentials in version control. No authentication on internal APIs. PII stored in plaintext. Compliance certifications claimed but never completed.

‚ö†

Engineering Theatre

Impressive demos backed by non-scalable architecture. "Microservices" that are a single monolith with extra steps. Test coverage numbers that exclude critical paths.

‚ö†

Hidden Dependencies

Critical functionality dependent on a single engineer. Undocumented reliance on deprecated third-party services. Open source licenses incompatible with commercial use.
